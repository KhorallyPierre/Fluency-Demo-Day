About Fluency

"The limits of my language mean the limits of my world. "

Speak with native speakers from the comfort of your couch

I myself grew to learn three languages in my 26 years. When I migrated to the United States at the age of 9, I recieved an opportunity to live in a country that would ensure my fluency in English. I later studied Spanish in college, and studied abroad to Spain and Mexico, language learning remained fun and accessible to me. Unfortunately not many can experience the privileges I had growing up,

especially during this time with COVID-19. This is where TalK comes in handy. Through TalK, my mission is to provide those who cannot travel for various reasons the opportunity to speak with natives of the language that they desire to learn. The idea is to simplify the language learning experience by pairing you up with a fluent individual. To learn more, Sign Up . Happy learning!
